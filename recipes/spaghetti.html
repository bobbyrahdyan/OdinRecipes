Spaghetti

Description

The most delicious spaghetti recipes we ever know. Happy cooking!!

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti;
  • Tomato sauce;
  • Ground beef;

Steps:

  1. heat 500 ml water in pot until boiling;
  2. Put the spaghetti in the pot for about 10 minutes;
  3. Fry the tomato souce with the ground beef;
  4. Put the sauce and spaghetti together;
