Fried Rice

Description

This thai fried rice recipes is a family secret from our ancestor for a long time. The rice, chicken, tofu, and other ingredients blend in to create an exquisite flavor. Happy cooking!!

Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice;
  • Chicken breast;
  • Tofu;
  • Soy sauce;
  • Lentil;
  • Butter;

Steps:

  1. Put basmati rice and soy sauce in frying pan;
  2. Stir it for 10 minutes;
  3. Put tofu and lentil in;
  4. Add chicken breast meat the last;
  5. put 100gr butter additional seasoning;
