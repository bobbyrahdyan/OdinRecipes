Fried Tofu

Description

The most delicious fried tofu recipes we ever know. Happy cooking!!

Ingredients:

  • Tofu;
  • Cornstarch;
  • Oil;
  • Soy sauce;

Steps:

  1. Put oil in frying pan and heat it up for about 7 minutes;
  2. Pliced tofu into small bit;
  3. Put the tofu into cornstarch;
  4. Put the tofu into fying pan;
  5. fry it until the color turn light brown;
